Explore Kentucky With Rewind Kentucky

Making exploring historical landmarks exciting, accessible, and informative.

What We Do

Rewind Kentucky is a local busniess that changes the way that historical knowledge is passed down and percieved through our patented, modern process. Here is the basics...

Landmarks

There are hundreds of historical landmarks across kentucky that most people don't know anything about. Rewind Kentucky adds a new level of interaction and modernization through our product.

Digital Profiles

Digital profiles are your gateway to a more in-depth look at the landmarks across the state. These digital profiles contain new and old photos of the landmarks to give a visual representaion of its history.

Interactive QR Codes

In order to access the "Digital Profile" for each marker, you will have to scan a QR code attached to the marker. Once you scan the QR code you can access to the "Digital Profile" where you can read about visualize the history and significance of the landmark.

Local Partnerships

Rewind Kentucky continously seeks partnerships with local cities, parks, tourism boards, chambers of commerce, and more. By doing so we can offer deeper insights into locally-held historical knowledge to visitors and offer valuable insights for local authorities.

Not only is Rewind Kentucky an exciting historical exploration catalyst, but with the hard work of the "Average Joes" that run it, Rewind Kentucky makes previously non-accommodating historical sites accessible to those with visual or mobility concerns.
